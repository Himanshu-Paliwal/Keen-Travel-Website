Places Covered :  
  • Kathmandu

  • Sightseeing

Daywise Tour Itinerary

Day 1 Arrival at Kathmandu

On the very first day, you will be welcomed to the country by our staff at the hotel. You will then be transferred to your hotel where you can spend some time relaxing in your room and getting rid of all the travel exhaustion. You will have the rest of the day off to explore the city. and its crowded streets and rich cultural diversity.

Day 2 Sightseeing in Kathmandu

On the second day, you will have a sumptuous breakfast in the morning before heading out for a sightseeing tour. Visit the Machhendra Nath Temple, a two-tiered bronze roof pagoda for a spiritual experience and check out Hanuman Shoka for a royal feel Kathmandu is home to a large number of medieval temples like Durbar Square, Temple house of Kumari, Pashupatinath Temple, and the temple of Lord Shiva, all of which you will cover on this day

Day 3 Kathmandu to Pokhara

After breakfast at the hotel, you will drive to Pokhara.

Day 4 Departure

After breakfast, you will catch your flight back home.
